Raffle Tickets

We'll be donating all money raised tonight to Mind and NHS Charities Toghether

This applies to all raffle tickets and donations received through our Online Store. We'll be splitting it 50:50 between these two charities:

Mind.org.uk - The Mental Health Charity

During these worrying and isolating times, mental wellbeing can suffer. Mind provide help and advice to anyone experiencing a mental health problem, as well as campaigning to improve services, raise awareness and promote understanding.

Regardless of who you are, or what you're going through, Mind are always there for anyone who needs help.

You can donate directly to Mind here

NHS Charities Together

While we stay home quizzing, the NHS is dealing with a crisis like none it has never experienced before. The doctors, nurses, staff and volunteers are selflessly working flat out to save lives.

NHS Charities Together is there to help the frontline of the NHS. By covering expenses for volunteers, bringing food and wash kits for staff working through the night, and keeping isolated patients in touch with their loved ones
